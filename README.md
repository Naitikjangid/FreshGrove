## 📌 About the Project
FreshGrove is a fully interactive single-page website designed 
to help first-year college students overcome the challenges of 
transitioning from school to college life.

Built as part of the **Design Thinking Capstone** project at 
NIET Greater Noida under subject **CASCC0201**.

The project follows the 5 stages of Design Thinking:
**Empathize → Define → Ideate → Prototype → Test**

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement
*"How Might We help first-year students successfully navigate 
the emotional, academic, and social challenges of transitioning 
from school to college?"*

**SDG Goals Aligned:**
- 🟢 SDG 3 — Good Health & Well-being
- 🔴 SDG 4 — Quality Education

---

## 📚 What's Inside the Website

| Section | Description |
|---|---|
| 📖 Guidebook | 6 major challenges with research-backed solutions |
| 🍽️ Food & Nutrition | Tips, 6 student recipes & nearby spots in Greater Noida |
| 💪 Health & Fitness | Exercise, sleep, wellness & campus health |
| ⏰ Time Management | Weekly planner + Pomodoro timer |
| 🫂 Social & Emotional | Imposter syndrome, homesickness, daily affirmations |
| 💰 Finance | Budget calculator + financial advice blogs |

---

## ✨ Features
- 🌿 Nature / Forest theme with animated floating leaves
- 🎵 Ambient forest background music
- 🖱️ Custom cursor
- 📊 Live budget calculator
- ⏰ Working Pomodoro focus timer
- 📅 Interactive weekly planner
- 📍 Real nearby food spots (Greater Noida — Alpha 1, KP2, KP3)
- 📺 YouTube videos with research citations
- 📱 Mobile responsive
